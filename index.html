Welcome


The Willimantic Orchestra is a non-profit community orchestra dedicated to the performance of symphonic orchestral music. The orchestra is loosely affiliated with the Music Program at Eastern Connecticut State University.

The concert season runs from September through May.  If you have any questions or are interested in becoming a member, please contact us at willimantic.orch@gmail.com.

The orchestra rehearses from 7pm to 9pm on Thursday nights (during concert season) at E. O. Smith High School in Storrs, CT. 

Performances are held at the Fine Arts Instructional Center (FAIC) building on campus at Eastern CT State University in Willimantic (on the linked map - it is building #32). Concert admission is free; contributions are gratefully accepted.
